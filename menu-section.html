Ghormeh Sabzi, A Taste of Persian Comfort!

A rich and aromatic herb stew made with fresh greens, tender meat, red kidney beans, and dried limes, slow-cooked to perfection for a deep, authentic flavor. A true Persian classic that brings warmth and tradition to every bite.

Fesenjan, A Rich Persian Delight!

A luscious and aromatic stew made with ground walnuts and pomegranate molasses, slow-cooked to a glossy, flavorful perfection. Traditionally served with tender chicken, it can also be made with savory meatballs for a hearty twist, bringing a perfect balance of sweet and tangy flavors to every bite.

Gheymeh, A Classic Persian Stew!

A hearty and flavorful stew made with tender meat, yellow split peas, and tomato-based sauce, slowly cooked to create a rich and comforting taste. Traditionally garnished with crispy potato sticks, it pairs perfectly with saffron rice and fresh herbs. This beloved Persian dish can also be enjoyed with meatballs for a delightful variation.

Sholeh Mashhadi, A Hearty Persian Porridge!

A rich and nourishing Persian dish made with a blend of tender meat, rice, lentils, and aromatic spices, slowly simmered to a creamy, porridge-like consistency. Traditionally garnished with cinnamon, fried onions, and nuts, it’s a comforting and wholesome meal, perfect for sharing with family and friends.
